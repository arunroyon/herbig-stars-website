An academic collaboration dedicated to multiwavelength studies of Herbig Ae/Be stars.

Welcome

This collaboration integrates observations from ultraviolet, optical, infrared, radio, and X-ray astronomy, along with theoretical modeling, to explore the fascinating phenomena around Herbig Ae/Be stars.
